Welcome! This is my first post as part of a Professional Development course at CU Boulder. I'll be giving a weekly update that follows the following format:

    What did you do last week?
    What do you plan to do this week?
    Are there any impediments in your way?
    Reflection on the process you used last week, how can you make the process work better?

  This week, I set up my github blog to document my progress throughout the course, as well as other projects I might be working on. I also started thinking through what my project for the course will be. I've narrowed it down to creating a visualize app for various algorithms that are used in the CSPB 3104 - Algorithms course. I'm inclined to include a couple of easy algorithms but also want to focus on some of the more complex ones such as Graphs.

  Impediments: No major impediments this week, trying to decide on the scope of the project and which algorithms to include.

  Reflection: Setting clear expectations and goals for the project will be important to getting things done week by week. For this coming week I want to finalize the project scope, tech stack, and put together a project plan with milestones and deadlines.
